Frequently asked questions

Are pets allowed?

Pets are not allowed in order to preserve comfort for all guests.

Is parking easy?

Yes, parking is available in front of the chalet. The plot is not fenced yet.

Is linen included?

Bed linen and towels are optional. You may also bring your own.

How does arrival work?

Self check-in is possible with a key box. Instructions are sent before your stay.
